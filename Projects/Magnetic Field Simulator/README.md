# Magnetic Field Simulator

An interactive client-side physics simulator modeling magnetic fields generated by various sources on an HTML5 canvas. It allows users to place, rotate, and resize magnetic dipoles, solenoids, and current-carrying wires, tracing field vectors, field lines, and the trajectories of charged particles moving under the Lorentz force.

## Features

- **Multi-Source Sandbox**: Place and manipulate sources:
  - **Bar Magnet**: Modeled as a magnetic dipole with North (Cyan) and South (Red) poles. Adjustable position, strength, and angle.
  - **Current Wire**: Infinite wire perpendicular to screen, creating circular fields. Toggle direction (in/out of page).
  - **Solenoid**: A sequence of coils generating uniform internal magnetic fields.
- **Dynamic Compass Sensor Probe**: Drag a compass probe around the canvas to see the needle align with the local field direction and read the field strength $B_x, B_y, |B|$ in Gauss.
- **Lorentz Force Particle Tracer**: Inject charged particles (electrons, protons) with custom initial velocity, mass, and charge. The simulator integrates $F = q(\vec{v} \times \vec{B})$ to animate their curved trajectories.
- **Interactive Visualizers**: Toggle between Field Line curves, Grid Vector arrows, and Particle Trails.
- **Controls & Diagnostics**: Live readouts of active components, simulation FPS, and Biot-Savart calculations.
- **Premium Glassmorphic Panel Layout**: Sleek dark theme using Outfit and JetBrains Mono typography.

## Run it

Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.

## Physics Equations Modeled

- **Magnetic Dipole Field**:
  $$B(\vec{r}) = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi} \frac{3(\vec{m} \cdot \hat{r})\hat{r} - \vec{m}}{r^3}$$
- **Biot-Savart Law (Infinite Wire)**:
  $$B = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2\pi r}$$
- **Lorentz Force**:
  $$\vec{F} = q(\vec{v} \times \vec{B})$$
