# Emergency Contact & Information Vault

A responsive Emergency Contact & Information Vault that allows you to securely store and manage essential personal and emergency-related information.

## Features
- **Personal Profile**: Store full name, DOB, blood group, address, and notes.
- **Emergency Contacts**: Add primary and secondary emergency contacts.
- **Medical Info**: Record medical conditions, allergies, and current medications.
- **Insurance Details**: Keep track of insurance providers, policy numbers, and emergency claim contacts.
- **Important Documents**: Keep a registry of identification records like Passport and Driver's License.
- **Emergency Dashboard**: A quick-access view to instantly get critical information when seconds matter.
- **Data Persistence**: Information is stored securely in your browser's Local Storage.

## How to use
Simply double-click `index.html` to open the vault in your browser. Fill out your information safely - it stays entirely on your local machine.
