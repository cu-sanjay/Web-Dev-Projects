Conservation Success Stories

Deforestation is critical, but active restoration campaigns have achieved remarkable recoveries. Explore inspiring reforestation campaigns below:

Costa Rica's Forest Doubling

Through payments for ecosystem services (PES) and national reserves, Costa Rica became the first tropical country to reverse deforestation, doubling its forest cover from 26% in the 1980s to over 52% today.

Reforestation: +26% Cover Impact: Ecotourism Leader

The Loess Plateau Restoration (China)

One of the largest ecological rehabilitation programs in the world transformed an arid, heavily eroded plateau the size of Belgium into a thriving agricultural and forested landscape, lifting 2.5 million people out of poverty.

Restored Land: 35,000 km² Impact: 100M+ tons sediment runoff reduced

Africa's Great Green Wall

An ambitious land restoration project running across the entire width of Africa's Sahel region, aiming to restore 100 million hectares of degraded land, capture 250 million tonnes of carbon, and create millions of green jobs.

Goal: 100M hectares restored Timeline: Target 2030 completion
