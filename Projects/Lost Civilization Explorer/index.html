Mayan Civilization

📅 Advanced calendar system
🔤 Hieroglyphic writing
🏛️ Pyramid architecture
🔢 Concept of zero

Overview

The Maya civilization flourished in Mesoamerica, known for its hieroglyphic script, advanced mathematics, and astronomical systems.

Key Achievements

  • Developed the only fully developed writing system in pre-Columbian Americas
  • Created accurate calendars based on astronomical observations
  • Built monumental pyramids and cities

Mysteries

The Classic Maya collapse around 900 CE remains debated — causes include drought, overpopulation, deforestation, and political instability.
