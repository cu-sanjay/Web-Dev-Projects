# AI Research Paper Explorer

Browse and search a curated collection of 12 landmark AI research papers. Filter by category (NLP, Computer Vision, RL, Generative AI, Theory) and year, with keyword search across titles, authors, and abstracts.

## Features
- 12 landmark AI papers with abstracts
- Category and year filters
- Keyword search
- Clickable paper cards with detail view

## Local Deployment
```
cd "AI Research Paper Explorer"
start index.html
```
